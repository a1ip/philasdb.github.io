Golden Living Center

7310 Stenton Ave. Phila. Pa 19150


We provide music for praise and worship and conduct a preaching ministry to the residents of the Golden Living Center. We have been involved with this ministry for 10 years, and we usually have approx. 30 residents in attendance each week. We also visit with some of the people who are bedridden, since they are unable to attend the church services. It is a non-denominational ministry based on preaching the Word of God for the edification of the saints and evangelizing the lost.
